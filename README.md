# Smash Squad

## Welcome to Smash Squad!

This is a King-of-the-Hill style two person PvP style game made in Unity 3D.

The aim of the game is to use power ups to knock your opponent off specially designed maps. Each player must pick a class which will consist off:

- One special ability:
  - Launch - Create an explosion at your feet, sending you and anyone close by flying into the air.
  - Warp - Obtain a projectile that allows you to warp around the map.
  - Ninja - Obtain the ability to turn transparent and move quicker to hunt your target.
  * Geomancer - Obtain the ability to summon small islands to hurl at your opponent, or ride on.

- One melee weapon designed to knock you back far.
- One ranged weapon designed to knock players back from a distance (although with less effectiveness than the melee weapon)

After 5 minutes of play time, fireballs start raining from the sky, exploding and knocking you around with high knockback.

This game is third person.
